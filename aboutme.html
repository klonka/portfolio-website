My Story

I am a UX/UI Designer living in (& loving) Berlin where I am on a mission to intertwine my creativity, curiosity and empathy into meaningful work. My goal as a UX/UI Designer is to create effective and purposeful designs through creative solutions to the root problem users are faced with.
